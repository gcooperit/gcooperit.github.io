About Me

I’m a freelance IT specialist and web developer with a background spanning computer information systems, software development, and infrastructure management. I work with small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals across Maryland who need reliable, personalized tech support without the overhead of a large firm.

My work covers a wide range: setting up and maintaining servers and networks, building and redesigning websites, creating custom software tools, managing cloud systems, and providing hands-on tech support and training. I approach every engagement practically — focused on what actually solves the problem.

Alongside IT work, I have skills in media content creation, documentation writing, AV systems management, and GIS mapping for data analysis tasks.

Process

How It Works

Whether it’s a quick fix or a larger project, here’s what working together looks like from start to finish.

01
We Talk Through Your Needs

Reach out by email or phone and we’ll have a conversation about what you’re dealing with. No commitment required — just a chance to figure out what actually needs to happen.

02
Proposal or Estimate (If Needed)

For larger or more complex projects, I can put together a written project proposal outlining options, scope, and estimated cost so you can make an informed decision.

03
Work Gets Done

I show up — on-site, remote, or both — and take care of the job. If additional needs come up along the way, we handle those too.

04
Invoice & Services Document

After the work is complete, you receive a detailed invoice and a services rendered document outlining exactly what was done. This gives you a clear record to keep on file or share with stakeholders.

05
Payment Within 30 Days

Invoices are due within 30 days of issue. No surprise fees — everything is agreed upon before work begins.

FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

I primarily serve Harford and Baltimore County, Maryland. I’m open to working in surrounding counties as well — reach out and we can discuss whether on-site work is feasible for your location. Remote work is available to anyone regardless of location.

Do you offer ongoing support contracts or retainers?

Yes. If you’d like to have me on retainer or set up regular monthly or yearly checkups on your business systems, I’m happy to work out an arrangement. Get in touch and we can discuss what makes sense for your needs.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

I typically respond within 24–48 hours depending on my current schedule. Availability is on a first come, first served basis unless a contract states otherwise.

Do you provide a quote before starting work?

For larger or more complex projects I can put together a full project proposal outlining options, scope, and estimated cost. For straightforward work, scope and cost are discussed before anything begins.

What happens after the work is done?

You’ll receive a detailed invoice and a services rendered document outlining exactly what was completed. Invoices are due within 30 days of issue.

Can you help if I don’t know what I need?

Absolutely. Feel free to reach out and describe what you’re dealing with — even if you’re not sure what the problem is or what to ask for. Part of what I do is help figure that out.
